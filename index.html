Why Use Soapberry as an Anti-bacterial body wash/soap?

Using soapberry as an antibacterial body wash offers a natural, eco-friendly, and gentle approach to maintaining clean, healthy, and well-hydrated skin. It is particularly appealing for individuals seeking a natural and hypoallergenic alternative to traditional body washes, especially those with skin sensitivities.

Benefits

Gentle Cleansing: Soapberry-based body wash is known for its gentle cleansing properties. It effectively removes dirt, sweat, and impurities from the skin without causing irritation, making it suitable for sensitive skin.

Natural Antibacterial Properties: Soapberry contains natural antibacterial properties, which can help reduce the presence of bacteria on the skin's surface.

Moisturizing: Some soapberry-based body washes have natural moisturizing properties that can help keep the skin hydrated, reducing dryness and itching.

pH-Balancing: Soapberry is close to the skin's natural pH level, helping to maintain the skin's pH balance and support healthy skin.

Eco-Friendly: Using soapberry as an antibacterial body wash aligns with eco-friendly practices, as it is biodegradable and has a lower environmental impact compared to some synthetic alternatives.

Contents

Glycerin (Sodium Stearate), Sodium Laurate, SLES, Aqua, Glycerin, Sucrose, Propylene Glycol, Sorbitol, Sodium Citrate, Sodium Chloride and Citric Acid (Calamansi)

Caution!

External use only. If skin irritation occurs, discontinue use. Immediately rinse with water in case of contact with eyes. If irritation persists, consult a doctor.

Always use personal care products in accordance with their intended purpose and with consideration of your individual skin type and needs. If you have any concerns about the use of soapberry-based body wash or any other skincare product, consult a healthcare professional or dermatologist for personalized guidance.

Proper Way to Wash Your Hands

1. Wet your hands with clean, running water, turn off the tap, and apply our ZenSkin Soap.

2. Build lather by rubbing your hands together. Don’t forget to include the back of your hands, between your fingers, and under your nails.

3. Keep scrubbing for at least 20 seconds — sing Happy Birthday or do the Lifebuoy dance if you need a timer!

4. Rinse well and dry your hands using a clean towel. Air dry them if a towel is not available.
