Proper Way to Wash Your Hands

1. wet your hands with clean, running water, turn off the tap, and apply our ZenSkin Soap.

2. build lather by rubbing your hands together. Don’t forget to include the back of your hands, between your fingers, and under your nails.

3. keep scrubbing for at least 20 seconds — sing Happy Birthday or do the Lifebuoy dance if you need a timer!

4. rinse well and dry your hands using a clean towel. Air dry them if a towel is not available.
